Our Story

From the heart of Italy to the heart of the Twin Cities

A Culinary Journey

Founded in 2024 by Marco Rossi and Elena Bianchi, Twin Cities Italiano was born from a shared dream: to bring the authentic flavors of Italy—hand-tossed pizzas, creamy pastas, and slow-simmered sauces—to Minneapolis and Saint Paul. After years of family recipes and kitchen experiments, they opened our doors in the historic Warehouse District, infusing every dish with tradition and passion.

Our Philosophy

Locally Sourced

We partner with Minnesota farms for the freshest produce, cheeses, and meats—supporting our community, one meal at a time.

Time-Honored Recipes

Each sauce simmers for hours, and dough ferments overnight—authentic techniques passed down through generations.

Warm Hospitality

From the moment you walk in, our goal is to make you feel like family, sharing laughter and stories over great food.
